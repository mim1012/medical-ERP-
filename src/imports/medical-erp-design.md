Design a premium Korean medical ERP web admin system for medical device and medical supplies businesses.

The target users are male business owners, executives, operations managers, logistics managers, sales managers, and service engineers in the medical industry.

The design must feel:
- premium
- trustworthy
- masculine
- modern B2B enterprise
- highly organized
- data-driven
- serious and reliable

Avoid:
- flashy startup style
- playful ecommerce style
- colorful consumer app feeling
- overly rounded cute UI
- excessive gradients

Use this color palette:
- Primary navy #163A5F
- Dark navy #0F2942
- Slate blue #35556E
- Accent blue #5B8DB8
- Background #F4F7FA
- White #FFFFFF
- Border #D7DEE6
- Text primary #18212B
- Text secondary #5B6773
- Success #2E7D5B
- Warning #C58A2B
- Danger #B94A48

Typography style:
- Pretendard-like Korean enterprise style
- clear hierarchy
- bold page titles
- large KPI values
- elegant table typography

Layout:
- left dark sidebar
- top clean white header
- main dashboard with KPI cards, charts, alert panels, and data tables
- desktop-first
- responsive but business-oriented

Sidebar menu:
- 대시보드
- 거래처 관리
- 품목 관리
- 재고 관리
- 입고/발주 관리
- 출고/판매 관리
- 설치/A/S 관리
- 문서 관리
- 정산 관리
- 세금계산서 관리
- 알림 센터
- 사용자/권한 관리
- 로그/이력 관리

This medical ERP must visually include real medical business contents:
- hospital and clinic client management
- product master with model name, manufacturer, approval number
- lot number management
- serial number tracking
- expiration date management
- warehouse inventory
- consignment stock by hospital
- purchase orders and goods receiving
- sales and shipment records
- installation history for medical devices
- A/S and maintenance logs
- warranty expiration tracking
- document management for approvals, manuals, certificates, contracts
- settlement and unpaid balance management
- tax invoice status
- alert center for expiry, low stock, warranty, unpaid accounts, missing serials
- user roles and activity logs

Dashboard sections:
1. KPI cards
- 이번 달 매출
- 미수금 잔액
- 오늘 출고 예정
- A/S 접수 건수
- 유효기간 임박 품목
- 보증 만료 예정 장비

2. alert cards
- 로트 추적 필요 건
- 시리얼 등록 누락
- 재고 부족 품목
- 문서 만료 예정
- 세금계산서 미발행
- 회수/이상 이슈

3. charts
- 월별 매출 추이
- 거래처별 매출 순위
- 품목별 매출 비중
- 장비 설치 및 점검 일정

4. data tables
- 최근 출고 현황
- 최근 A/S 현황
- 최근 문서 업로드
- 최근 정산 현황

Use elegant line icons, premium enterprise cards, clean tables, status badges, subtle shadows, thin borders, and refined spacing.

Make the system feel like a high-value Korean medical ERP trusted by serious business owners.
